Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

The minimum investment is $250,000 USD. This threshold ensures alignment with our institutional-grade strategy and allows for meaningful participation in the fund's performance.

The AP7 model is based on systematic, rules-based investing with selective leverage on global indices. It prioritizes long-term returns through disciplined risk management and low-cost execution, avoiding market timing and emotional decisions.

CQT operates on a performance-only fee model. We only succeed when you succeed. There are no management fees - our compensation is entirely aligned with delivering returns above the benchmark.

CQT Fund operates under the DIFC (Dubai International Financial Centre) regulatory framework, one of the world's leading financial hubs with robust investor protections and international standards.

Our target is +20% annual percentage yield (APY). This is based on historical performance of the Swedish model methodology, with an ESMA Risk rating of 5 reflecting the growth-oriented strategy.
